## Highlights

### Monterey Bay Aquarium
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Monterey+Bay+Aquarium+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.montereybayaquarium.org)
- One of the world's best aquariums
- Sea otters, jellyfish, kelp forest, penguins
- Touch pools for kids
- Feeding shows throughout the day
- **Hours:** 10 AM - 5 PM
- **Cost:** ~$60 adults, $40 children (3-12), free under 3

> **Kid-Friendly:** THIS IS A MUST! The 5-year-old will be mesmerized by the otters and jellyfish. The 18-month-old will love the colorful fish. Touch pools are a huge hit. Plan at least 4 hours - you could spend all day!

**Aquarium Tips:**
- Buy tickets online in advance
- Arrive at opening (10 AM) for smaller crowds
- Feeding times are posted at entrance - plan around them
- Sea otter feeding: 10:30 AM, 1:30 PM, 3:30 PM
- Bring a stroller (lots of walking)
- Cafe inside serves decent food

---

### Dennis the Menace Playground
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Dennis+the+Menace+Playground+Monterey+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.monterey.org/Visitors/Dennis-the-Menace-Playground)
- Designed by Hank Ketcham (Dennis the Menace creator)
- Massive climbing structures
- Suspension bridge, roller slide
- Lake for paddle boats
- **Free!**
- **Hours:** 10 AM - dusk

> **Kid-Friendly:** One of the best playgrounds in California! Both kids will love it. Perfect for burning energy after the aquarium.

---

### Cannery Row
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Cannery+Row+Monterey+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.canneryrow.com)
- Historic waterfront street
- Made famous by John Steinbeck
- Restaurants, shops, galleries
- Walk along the coastal path
- Aquarium is located here

> **Kid-Friendly:** Fun to walk, ice cream shops, street performers on weekends. Good for evening strolls.

---

### Carmel Beach
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Carmel+Beach+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.carmelcalifornia.com/carmel-beach.htm)
- White sand beach
- Dogs allowed (off-leash!)
- Beautiful cypress tree backdrop
- Sunset watching
- **Free parking** at bottom of Ocean Avenue

> **Kid-Friendly:** Fantastic beach for playing! Soft white sand, gentle waves. The 5-year-old can run and play; bring buckets and shovels for the 18-month-old.

---

### 17-Mile Drive
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=17+Mile+Drive+Pebble+Beach+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.pebblebeach.com/17-mile-drive/)
- Famous scenic coastal drive
- Lone Cypress, Bird Rock, Pebble Beach golf
- Designated stops with parking
- **Cost:** $11.75/vehicle

**Key Stops:**

| Stop | Highlight |
|------|-----------|
| Bird Rock Vista | Sea lions and birds |
| Fanshell Beach | White sand, seals |
| Cypress Point Lookout | Dramatic rocky coast |
| The Lone Cypress | Most photographed tree! |
| Pebble Beach | Famous golf course |

> **Kid-Friendly:** Beautiful drive with lots of stops. Kids will love seeing the sea lions at Bird Rock. The Lone Cypress is a quick photo stop.

---

### Carmel Village
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Carmel+by+the+Sea+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.carmelcalifornia.com)
- Charming fairytale village
- Art galleries, boutiques
- No street addresses (part of the charm!)
- Pet-friendly town

> **Kid-Friendly:** Nice for a short stroll. Ice cream, bakeries, and window shopping. The beach is the main attraction for kids.

---

### Fisherman's Wharf Monterey
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Fishermans+Wharf+Monterey+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.montereywharf.com)
- Historic working wharf
- Seafood restaurants
- Sea lions underneath!
- Whale watching tours depart here

> **Kid-Friendly:** Similar to SF's but smaller. Good for clam chowder and watching boats.

---

### Tide Pools (Pacific Grove)
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lovers+Point+Pacific+Grove+CA)
- At Lovers Point and Asilomar State Beach
- Best at low tide
- Sea stars, anemones, crabs
- **Check tide tables!**

> **Kid-Friendly:** Amazing for curious kids! Check tide times and go during low tide. Gentle supervision needed near slippery rocks.
