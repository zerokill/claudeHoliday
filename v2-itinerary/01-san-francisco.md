## Highlights

### San Francisco Zoo
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=San+Francisco+Zoo+California)
- [Official Website](https://www.sfzoo.org)
- Over 2,000 animals including gorillas, penguins, and koalas
- Little Puffer miniature steam train
- Fisher Family Children's Zoo (petting area)
- Playground inside the zoo
- **Hours:** 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM daily
- **Cost:** ~$25 adults, $18 children (4-14), free under 3

> **Kid-Friendly:** Perfect for both ages! Petting zoo, train ride, playground. Plan 2-3 hours minimum.

---

### Fisherman's Wharf & Pier 39
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pier+39+San+Francisco+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.pier39.com)
- Sea lions at K-Dock (free viewing!)
- Aquarium of the Bay (touch pools)
- Street performers, carousel
- Sourdough bread bowls at Boudin Bakery

> **Kid-Friendly:** Sea lions are a huge hit! Carousel, street performers, and food options everywhere.

---

### Golden Gate Bridge
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Golden+Gate+Bridge+San+Francisco+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.goldengate.org)
- Walk or bike across (2.7 km each way)
- Photo spots on both sides
- Battery Spencer viewpoint (north side, great for photos)
- Golden Gate Bridge Welcome Center (south side)

> **Kid-Friendly:** A short walk partway across is doable with stroller. Great for photos. Windy - bring layers!

---

### Crissy Field
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Crissy+Field+San+Francisco+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.parksconservancy.org/parks/crissy-field)
- Beach with Golden Gate views
- Flat paths perfect for strollers
- Playground near the Warming Hut
- Kite flying popular here

> **Kid-Friendly:** Beach play, playground, flat walking. Great picnic spot with bridge views!

---

### Exploratorium
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Exploratorium+San+Francisco+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.exploratorium.edu)
- 600+ interactive exhibits
- Tactile Dome (advance reservation)
- Bay Observatory with views
- **Hours:** 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (closed Mon)
- **Cost:** ~$30 adults, $25 children (4-17), free under 4

> **Kid-Friendly:** Incredible for the 5-year-old! Touch everything, experiment, play. The 18-month-old will enjoy the colorful exhibits and open spaces. Plan 2-3 hours.

---

### Cable Cars
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Powell+Street+Cable+Car+Turnaround+San+Francisco+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.sfmta.com/getting-around/muni/cable-cars)
- Historic moving landmarks
- Powell-Hyde line has best views
- **Cost:** $8 per ride
- **Tip:** Board at intermediate stops to avoid long Powell Street lines

> **Kid-Friendly:** A thrill for kids! Hold on tight, stand on the outside. Short ride, big memories.

> **Kid-Friendly:** One of the best playgrounds in the city! The concrete slides are legendary.

## Practical Information

### Getting Around
- **Rental car:** Pick up at SFO, useful for entire trip
- **Parking:** Can be expensive and limited downtown
- **Tip:** Use hotel parking or park outside downtown and use transit/Uber for city days

### Restaurants with Kids

**Boudin Bakery at Fisherman's Wharf**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Boudin+Bakery+Fisherman+Wharf+San+Francisco+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.boudinbakery.com)
- Famous sourdough bread bowls
- Kid-friendly, casual

**In-N-Out Burger (Fisherman's Wharf)**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=In+N+Out+Burger+Fisherman+Wharf+San+Francisco)
- [Official Website](https://www.in-n-out.com)
- California classic
- Simple menu, fresh food

### Baby Facilities
- Most attractions have changing facilities
- Exploratorium has a family restroom
- Zoo has multiple family restrooms

### Weather Tips
- San Francisco is cool and foggy, especially mornings
- Dress in layers
- Afternoon can be sunny and warmer
- April averages: 12-18°C
