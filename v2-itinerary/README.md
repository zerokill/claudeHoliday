## Detailed Itinerary

### Days 1-3 | San Francisco (Apr 17-19)
[View detailed plan](01-san-francisco.md)

**Location:** San Francisco, California
**Drive:** Arrival day
**Nights:** 3

---

### Days 4-7 | Yosemite National Park (Apr 20-23)
[View detailed plan](02-yosemite.md)

**Location:** Yosemite Valley, California
**Drive:** 3-4 hours from San Francisco (~310 km)
**Nights:** 4

**Highlights:**
- Yosemite Falls (peak flow in April!)
- Valley View & Tunnel View
- Mirror Lake (easy walk)
- El Capitan & Half Dome views

> **Kid-Friendly:** Valley shuttle, short nature walks, dramatic waterfalls, playgrounds

**Note:** Peak waterfall season - April is one of the best times to visit!

---

### Days 8-10 | Las Vegas (Apr 24-26)
[View detailed plan](03-las-vegas.md)

**Location:** Las Vegas, Nevada
**Drive:** 7-8 hours from Yosemite (~580 km)
**Nights:** 3

**Highlights:**
- Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area
- Bellagio Fountains
- Fremont Street Experience
- Hotel pools

> **Kid-Friendly:** Red Rock scenic drive, fountain shows, hotel pools, Shark Reef Aquarium

---

### Days 11-13 | Zion National Park (Apr 27-29)
[View detailed plan](04-zion.md)

**Location:** Springdale, Utah
**Drive:** 2.5-3 hours from Las Vegas (~260 km)
**Nights:** 3

**Highlights:**
- Zion Canyon Scenic Drive
- Riverside Walk (paved, stroller-friendly!)
- Emerald Pools Trail (easy)
- Canyon Junction viewpoints

> **Kid-Friendly:** Shuttle system, easy walking trails, river wading, playgrounds in Springdale

---

### Days 14-16 | Grand Canyon South Rim (Apr 30-May 2)
[View detailed plan](05-grand-canyon.md)

**Location:** Grand Canyon Village, Arizona
**Drive:** 4.5 hours from Zion (~410 km via Kanab/Page)
**Nights:** 3

**Highlights:**
- Mather Point sunrise
- Rim Trail (paved, stroller-friendly sections)
- Grand Canyon Village
- Desert View Watchtower
- IMAX Theatre

> **Kid-Friendly:** Paved Rim Trail, Junior Ranger program, IMAX movie, wildlife spotting

**Note:** Scenic drive through Kanab and past Lake Powell/Page!

---

### Day 17 | Sequoia National Park (May 3)
[View detailed plan](06-sequoia.md)

**Location:** Three Rivers, California (gateway town)
**Drive:** 6 hours from Grand Canyon (~550 km)
**Nights:** 1 (Three Rivers)

**Highlights:**
- General Sherman Tree (world's largest!)
- Tunnel Log (drive through a fallen giant sequoia)
- Big Trees Trail (easy, paved)
- Giant Forest Museum

> **Kid-Friendly:** Giant trees are magical! Easy paved trails, drive-through log

---

### Days 18-20 | Monterey & Carmel (May 4-6)
[View detailed plan](07-monterey.md)

**Location:** Monterey/Carmel, California
**Drive:** 4-5 hours from Sequoia (~280 km)
**Nights:** 3

**Highlights:**
- Monterey Bay Aquarium (world-class!)
- 17-Mile Drive
- Carmel Beach
- Dennis the Menace Playground
- Cannery Row

> **Kid-Friendly:** Aquarium is a MUST, incredible beach playgrounds, tide pools

---

### Day 21 | Departure (May 7)

**Drive:** 2 hours from Monterey to SFO Airport (~190 km)
**Departure:** San Francisco International Airport (SFO)

## National Parks Pass - HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

### America the Beautiful Annual Pass

**Cost:** $80 (valid for 12 months)

**Your Trip Breakdown:**

| Park | Individual Entry Fee | With Annual Pass |
|------|---------------------|------------------|
| **Yosemite National Park** | $35/car (7 days) | Included |
| **Zion National Park** | $35/car (7 days) | Included |
| **Grand Canyon National Park** | $35/car (7 days) | Included |
| **Sequoia National Park** | $35/car (7 days) | Included |
| **Total Without Pass** | **$140** | - |
| **Total With Pass** | - | **$80** |

**Why Get It:** You save $60 AND get flexibility for spontaneous stops!

**Where to Buy:**
- [USGS Store Official](https://store.usgs.gov/recreational-passes) - Ships within 5-10 business days
- At any park entrance gate

## Essential Preparations

### Before You Go:
- [ ] Book flights to/from San Francisco
- [ ] Reserve rental car (SUV recommended)
- [ ] Book accommodations (especially Yosemite & Zion)
- [ ] Purchase National Parks Annual Pass
- [ ] Download offline maps for parks
- [ ] Pack car entertainment for kids

### What to Pack:
- Comfortable hiking shoes
- Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
- Reusable water bottles
- Hats and sunglasses
- Stroller (good for paved trails)
- Road trip games/tablets for kids
- First aid kit
- Camera/phone chargers
- Layers for Yosemite/Sequoia (cooler at elevation)
