## National Parks Pass - HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

### America the Beautiful Annual Pass

**Cost:** $80 (valid for 12 months)

**Your Trip Breakdown:**

| Park | Individual Entry Fee | With Annual Pass |
|------|---------------------|------------------|
| **Yosemite National Park** | $35/car (7 days) | Included |
| **Zion National Park** | $35/car (7 days) | Included |
| **Grand Canyon National Park** | $35/car (7 days) | Included |
| **Total Without Pass** | **$105** | - |
| **Total With Pass** | - | **$80** |

**Why Get It:** You save $25 AND get flexibility for spontaneous stops!

**Where to Buy:**
- [USGS Store Official](https://store.usgs.gov/recreational-passes) - Ships within 5-10 business days
- At any park entrance gate

## Essential Preparations

### Before You Go:
- [ ] Book flights to/from San Francisco
- [ ] Reserve rental car (SUV recommended)
- [ ] Book accommodations (especially Yosemite, Zion & Grand Canyon)
- [ ] Purchase National Parks Annual Pass
- [ ] Check Tioga Pass status (may be closed in early May)
- [ ] Download offline maps for parks
- [ ] Pack car entertainment for kids

### What to Pack:
- Comfortable hiking shoes
- Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
- Reusable water bottles
- Hats and sunglasses
- Stroller (good for paved trails)
- Road trip games/tablets for kids
- First aid kit
- Camera/phone chargers
- Warm layers for Eastern Sierra (cooler at elevation)
