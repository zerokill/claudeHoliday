## Highlights

### Yosemite Falls
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Yosemite+Falls+Yosemite+National+Park+CA)
- [NPS Info](https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/yosemitefalls.htm)
- North America's tallest waterfall (739m total)
- Peak flow in April-May from snowmelt
- Lower Yosemite Fall walk: 1.6 km paved loop
- Upper Yosemite Fall hike: NOT recommended with kids (strenuous)

> **Kid-Friendly:** Lower Yosemite Fall is perfect - short, paved, dramatic! Bring rain jackets for mist.

---

### Bridalveil Fall
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bridalveil+Fall+Yosemite+National+Park+CA)
- [NPS Info](https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/bridalveilfall.htm)
- 189m waterfall
- Short walk to viewing area (~0.8 km round trip)
- Very misty in April - you will get wet!

> **Kid-Friendly:** Short walk, dramatic falls. Kids love the mist! Bring waterproof jackets.

---

### Tunnel View
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Tunnel+View+Yosemite+National+Park+CA)
- [NPS Info](https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/tunnelview.htm)
- Most iconic viewpoint in Yosemite
- El Capitan, Bridalveil Fall, Half Dome all visible
- Stop on your way into the valley

> **Kid-Friendly:** Quick photo stop, no walking required. Parking lot views!

---

### Mirror Lake
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mirror+Lake+Yosemite+National+Park+CA)
- [NPS Info](https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/mirrorlake.htm)
- Beautiful reflections in morning
- Easy flat loop walk (3.2 km)
- Stroller-friendly paved path
- Seasonal lake (best in spring)

> **Kid-Friendly:** Flat, easy walk. Great for strollers. Reflections are magical in morning light.

---

### El Capitan
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=El+Capitan+Meadow+Yosemite+National+Park+CA)
- [NPS Info](https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/elcapitan.htm)
- World's largest granite monolith (900m vertical face)
- Watch for climbers with binoculars
- El Capitan Meadow is the best viewing spot

> **Kid-Friendly:** Look for tiny climbers on the massive rock face! Bring binoculars.

---

### Valley View
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Valley+View+Yosemite+National+Park+CA)
- Iconic view of El Capitan, Bridalveil Fall, Cathedral Rocks
- Merced River in foreground
- Beautiful at sunrise/sunset

> **Kid-Friendly:** Roadside viewpoint, great for photos. No walking needed.

---

### Cook's Meadow Loop
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Cooks+Meadow+Yosemite+National+Park+CA)
- Easy 1.6 km boardwalk loop
- Views of Half Dome, Yosemite Falls, Sentinel Rock
- Interpretive signs about meadow ecology

> **Kid-Friendly:** Flat boardwalk, stroller-friendly, great for wildlife spotting.

## Practical Information

### Getting Around

**Free Shuttle System:**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Yosemite+Valley+Shuttle+Stop+1+CA)
- [NPS Shuttle Info](https://www.nps.gov/yose/planyourvisit/valleyshuttle.htm)
- Free, runs every 10-20 minutes
- Covers all main valley attractions
- Stroller-friendly, easy with kids
- Runs 7 AM - 10 PM (spring schedule)

**Parking:**
- Free throughout the park
- Yosemite Village lot fills early (by 9-10 AM on weekends)
- Use shuttle once parked

### Where to Eat

**Yosemite Valley Lodge Food Court**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Yosemite+Valley+Lodge+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.travelyosemite.com/lodging/yosemite-valley-lodge/)
- Casual cafeteria style
- Pizza, burgers, salads
- High chairs available

**The Ahwahnee Dining Room**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The+Ahwahnee+Hotel+Yosemite+CA)
- [Official Website](https://www.travelyosemite.com/lodging/the-ahwahnee/)
- Upscale option for a special dinner
- Reservations recommended
- Kids welcome (dress code: smart casual)

**Village Store / Deli**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Yosemite+Village+Store+CA)
- Groceries, deli sandwiches, snacks
- Good for picnic supplies

**Tip:** Picnics are the best way to eat in Yosemite with kids! Stock up at the Village Store.

### Baby Facilities
- Changing facilities at Visitor Center
- Family restrooms at Yosemite Lodge
- Nursing areas available

### Weather & What to Pack

**April Weather:**
- Mornings: 8-12°C (cool, possibly frost)
- Afternoons: 18-22°C (pleasant)
- Rain possible
- Waterfalls create mist - bring rain jackets

**Pack:**
- Layers (warm jacket for mornings)
- Rain jackets/ponchos
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Binoculars (for climbers, wildlife)
- Sunscreen and hats
- Picnic supplies

### Wildlife
- Bears are active - follow food storage rules!
- Deer are common in meadows
- Coyotes sometimes seen at dawn/dusk
- Squirrels everywhere (don't feed them!)

## Accommodation Tips

**In the Park:**
- Yosemite Valley Lodge (mid-range, good location)
- Curry Village (cabins and tent cabins)
- The Ahwahnee (luxury, historic)

**Book EARLY** - Yosemite accommodations fill months in advance!

**Outside the Park:**
- El Portal (8 km from valley): Yosemite View Lodge
- Mariposa (56 km): Budget motels, more availability
- Groveland (40 km): Small town, vacation rentals
