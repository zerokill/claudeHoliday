## Highlights

### Horseshoe Bend
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Horseshoe+Bend+Page+AZ)
- [Official Website](https://www.nps.gov/glca/planyourvisit/horseshoebend.htm)
- Iconic 270° bend in the Colorado River
- 1.2 km round trip walk from parking
- 300m drop to the river below
- Best light: Late afternoon
- **Cost:** $10 parking fee

> **Kid-Friendly Caution:** NO RAILINGS at the edge! Hold children's hands firmly. The 18-month-old should stay in a carrier. Beautiful but requires attention to safety. If nervous about heights, view from further back.

**Tips:**
- Go late afternoon for best photos (sun behind you)
- Bring water - no shade on the walk
- The walk is slightly uphill going, downhill returning
- Can be hot in afternoon - bring hats

---

### Glen Canyon Dam
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Glen+Canyon+Dam+Page+AZ)
- [Official Website](https://www.nps.gov/glca/planyourvisit/visitorcenters.htm)
- 220m high concrete dam
- Carl Hayden Visitor Center
- Views of Lake Powell and the dam
- Free to visit overlook
- Visitor Center has exhibits

> **Kid-Friendly:** The dam is impressive to see! Quick stop, good views. Visitor Center has restrooms.

---

### Lake Powell
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lake+Powell+Page+AZ)
- [Official Website](https://www.nps.gov/glca/)
- Second largest man-made reservoir in USA
- Red rock canyons and blue water
- Beaches accessible in some areas
- Wahweap Marina is closest to Page

> **Kid-Friendly:** If you have time, stop at Wahweap Beach for a quick play. Beautiful setting!

---

### Antelope Canyon (Optional)

**Upper Antelope Canyon**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Upper+Antelope+Canyon+Page+AZ)
- Famous slot canyon with light beams
- **REQUIRES guided tour** - book well in advance!
- Tours run ~1 hour
- Not ideal for young children (narrow, crowded, rush through)
- **Cost:** $80-150+ per person

**Lower Antelope Canyon**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lower+Antelope+Canyon+Page+AZ)
- Requires climbing ladders - NOT suitable for 18-month-old
- Book in advance if interested

> **Kid-Friendly Note:** With a 5-year-old and 18-month-old, Antelope Canyon is challenging. The tours are rushed, expensive, and not designed for young kids. **Recommendation:** Skip it this trip and enjoy Horseshoe Bend instead. You can always return when kids are older!

## Practical Information

### Getting Around
- Page is small and easy to navigate
- Horseshoe Bend is 6 km south of town
- Glen Canyon Dam is 3 km north of town

### Where to Eat

**Big John's Texas BBQ**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Big+Johns+Texas+BBQ+Page+AZ)
- Great BBQ, casual
- Family-friendly

**Slackers**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Slackers+Page+AZ)
- Burgers and American food
- Good for kids

**El Tapatio**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=El+Tapatio+Page+AZ)
- Mexican food
- Family-friendly

### Baby Facilities
- Most restaurants are family-friendly
- Glen Canyon Visitor Center has restrooms
- Horseshoe Bend parking has pit toilets

### Weather Tips
- Late April can be warm (25-30°C)
- Little shade at Horseshoe Bend
- Bring sunscreen, hats, water
- Afternoon sun is strong
