## Highlights

### Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Red+Rock+Canyon+National+Conservation+Area+Las+Vegas+NV)
- [Official Website](https://www.blm.gov/programs/national-conservation-lands/nevada/red-rock-canyon)
- 21 km scenic loop drive
- Stunning red sandstone formations
- Short hiking trails suitable for families
- Visitor Center with exhibits
- **Hours:** 6 AM - 7 PM (April)
- **Cost:** $15/vehicle (America the Beautiful pass accepted!)

> **Kid-Friendly:** The scenic drive is perfect with kids - stop at viewpoints, stretch legs, take photos. Go early before it gets hot!

**Easy Trails at Red Rock:**

**Calico Tanks Trail**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Calico+Tanks+Trail+Red+Rock+Canyon+NV)
- 4 km round trip, moderate
- Rock scrambling (fun for 5-year-old, carry 18-month-old in carrier)
- Best views in the park

**Moenkopi Loop**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Moenkopi+Loop+Trail+Red+Rock+Canyon+NV)
- 3 km loop, easy
- Flat, good for strollers on parts
- Desert wildlife and plants

---

### Bellagio Fountains
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bellagio+Fountains+Las+Vegas+NV)
- [Official Website](https://bellagio.mgmresorts.com/en/entertainment/fountains-of-bellagio.html)
- FREE water show choreographed to music
- 300m long lake with 1,200+ fountains
- Shows every 30 min (afternoons), every 15 min (evenings)
- **Hours:** 3 PM - midnight (Mon-Fri), Noon - midnight (Sat-Sun)

> **Kid-Friendly:** Absolutely magical! Kids love the water and music. Best viewed at dusk when lights come on.

---

### Shark Reef Aquarium
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Shark+Reef+Aquarium+Mandalay+Bay+Las+Vegas+NV)
- [Official Website](https://www.sharkreef.com)
- Sharks, rays, sea turtles, jellyfish
- Touch pool with rays
- Walk-through tunnel
- **Hours:** 10 AM - 8 PM
- **Cost:** ~$29 adults, $23 children (5-12), free under 5

> **Kid-Friendly:** Great alternative to outdoor activities in the heat. The 5-year-old will love the sharks; the 18-month-old will enjoy the colorful fish.

---

### Fremont Street Experience
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Fremont+Street+Experience+Las+Vegas+NV)
- [Official Website](https://vegasexperience.com)
- Covered pedestrian mall (5 blocks, air-conditioned!)
- World's largest video screen ceiling
- Live music and street performers
- Light show every hour after dark
- FREE to walk around

> **Kid-Friendly:** It's a covered, pedestrian-only street so safer and cooler than the Strip. The light show overhead is spectacular!

---

### Hotel Pools

Most Vegas hotels have amazing pool complexes. A few family-friendly options:

**Mandalay Bay Beach**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mandalay+Bay+Pool+Las+Vegas+NV)
- Real sand beach, wave pool, lazy river
- One of the best for kids

**Excalibur Pool**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Excalibur+Hotel+Pool+Las+Vegas+NV)
- Medieval themed
- Good for families, less crowded

**Circus Circus Splash Zone**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Circus+Circus+Pool+Las+Vegas+NV)
- Waterslides
- Very family-oriented

> **Kid-Friendly Tip:** Check if your hotel pool is open to outside guests (some charge day fees). Mandalay Bay has the best family pool complex in Vegas.

## Practical Information

### Getting Around
- **Rental car:** You'll have your road trip car
- **Parking:** Most Strip hotels charge $15-25/day for parking
- **Walking:** The Strip is walkable but HOT - use air-conditioned casino walkways
- **Monorail:** Runs behind the Strip, can be useful

### Where to Eat

**In-N-Out Burger**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=In+N+Out+Burger+Las+Vegas+Strip+NV)
- [Official Website](https://www.in-n-out.com)
- California/Nevada classic
- Location near the Strip

**Gordon Ramsay Fish & Chips**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Gordon+Ramsay+Fish+and+Chips+LINQ+Las+Vegas)
- At The LINQ Promenade
- Quick, casual, tasty

**Rainforest Cafe**
- [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rainforest+Cafe+Las+Vegas+NV)
- At MGM Grand
- Kids love the animatronic animals and thunderstorms

**Food Courts:**
- Most casino hotels have food courts with varied options
- High chairs usually available

### Baby Facilities
- All major hotels have family restrooms
- Changing facilities widely available
- Vegas is surprisingly family-friendly in the right places

### Weather Tips

**Late April Weather:**
- Daytime: 25-30°C (hot in sun)
- Evening: 15-20°C (pleasant)
- Very dry - stay hydrated!
- Strong sun - sunscreen essential

**Tips:**
- Do outdoor activities (Red Rock) in the morning
- Afternoons: pools or indoor attractions
- Evenings: Strip walks, fountain shows
