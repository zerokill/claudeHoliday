## üìã Booking Tips

### **How to Specify Vehicle:**

When booking, rental companies use **categories**, not specific models:

- **"Compact SUV"** = RAV4, CRV, etc. (too small!)
- **"Midsize SUV"** = Santa Fe, Edge, etc. (adequate)
- **"Standard SUV"** = Acadia, Explorer, etc. (**BEST**)
- **"Full-Size SUV"** = Tahoe, Expedition, etc. (may be too big/expensive)
- **"Minivan"** = Pacifica, Odyssey, etc. (**ALSO BEST**)

**Book:** "Standard SUV" or "Minivan"
